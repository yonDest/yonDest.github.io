About Me

Hello there! I'm Yoni, a Software Engineer. I enjoy building things, not just for the web - I like electrifying bikes, creating DIY projects for my garden, and building apps that bring my interests to life.

My fascination with technology began in 2009 when I discovered the neatness of keyboard shortcuts. Who knew that something as simple as Ctrl + W could spark an interest that would stay with me for years to come? As I delved deeper, I started experimenting with HTML on my school computer, playfully altering the website's content on my device to prank my friends. I'd change the text to something silly and then innocently ask, "Why is my screen saying this?" - always good for a laugh! This curiosity soon blossomed into a broader interest in tech, and I've been hooked ever since.

Fast forward to today and I have had the privilege of working in IT for my city's government office, a student-led formula race team, and a very large medical device company. As a software engineer, I'm excited to work on complex problems that require creative solutions. Outside of work, you can find me cheering on my favorite soccer teams, staying up-to-date on the latest finance and tech, or exploring the fascinating world of history.
